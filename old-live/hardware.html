Hardware Loans

Looking for Hardware? HackOHI/O, in coordination with Electronics Club and TechHub, is providing free hardware loans for students to use on their hacks. Hardware can be borrowed from the Help Desk in the Grand Ballroom near the stage.

Don't forget to take a valid ID with you.
